Welcome in the Universe of minecraft

Minecraft is much more than just a video game — it’s a boundless world of creativity, adventure, and endless possibilities. Created by Markus Persson and developed by Mojang Studios, this sandbox game drops players into a procedurally generated world made entirely of blocks to gather, craft, and build. Whether you enjoy construction, exploration, or survival challenges, Minecraft offers something for everyone, whether playing solo or with friends.

We will explore two different sides of the history of Minecraft here, the most technical part will talk of the development of this game, the second one is based on the different evolutions that the game had towards the years.
